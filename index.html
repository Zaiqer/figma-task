About the course

Sometimes films are more complex than four-volume novels. But they teach you to read books from school, but not to watch movies. However, without preparation it can be difficult to enjoy watching.

In this 8-lesson course we will learn to be conscious spectators. Let's get acquainted with the stages of film production and learn how to watch a movie through the eyes of a screenwriter, director and editor. Let's understand the features of the genres and consider the most important films, from “Citizen Kane” to “The Matrix”.
